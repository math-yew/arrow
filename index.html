HOW

A large portion of my art is 100% digitally made. There is still a bunch of my art that has started as an acrylic painting or pencil drawing but then finalized with editing on the computer.

WHY

I make art because ... I want to. Enough said right? A lot of my art is based on my Christian beliefs. I believe that Christ is the solution to any of our pain. So if I can point someone towards Him through my art, and help them access His healing, I can't think of anything more satisfying.

WHEN

Ever since I was a little kid I've always... oh wait, that's everyone else's story, not mine. For me, art has been a more recent passion. I strive to perfect my style. Despite how my portfolio has developed over time, expect that the best is yet to come.

WHERE

Instagram

You'll find pretty much everything I make on my Instagram account. Follow me on Instagram to see my latest projects.

If you find anything there that you really like but can't find on my Etsy store, tell me, and I'll look into getting it added for you.


Etsy

You can purchase my art on Etsy. My shop name is named LookUpward. I encourage you to click the 'Favorite Shop' once you visit so that you will be able to find my shop easier in the future.

Please consider my art if you are upgrading the look of your decor. It also makes for a good gift idea.
